# Kafka at Scale: Patterns that Survive

Scaling Kafka is an exercise in balancing throughput and durability. Success lies in fine-tuning partition strategies and mastering consumer rebalancing. When millions of messages flow per second, your architecture must be as fluid as the data itself.

## Lessons from the Trenches
1. **Partitioning**: Your key to parallelism.
2. **Replication**: The safety net.
3. **Consumer Groups**: Efficient distribution.

Mastering these patterns ensures your data pipeline survives the highest of loads.
